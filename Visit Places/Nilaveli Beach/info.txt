Nilaveli Beach (නිලාවේලි වෙරළ)


Why it’s Important (Significance)

*	Nilaveli is famous for its long stretch of golden sand and turquoise blue water. Unlike the southern coast, the sea here is often incredibly calm 	during the season, making it look like a giant swimming pool. It is a world-class destination for marine life enthusiasts and those looking for a 	peaceful getaway away from the city hustle.

What to See and Do

*	Pigeon Island National Park: This is the highlight of Nilaveli. You can take a 10-15 minute boat ride to this island, which is surrounded by a 			world-famous coral reef. It’s the best place in Sri Lanka for Snorkeling to see colorful fish, sea turtles, and even black-tip reef sharks.

*	Relaxing & Swimming: The beach is very shallow and the waves are gentle, making it perfect for a safe and relaxing swim.

*	Whale & Dolphin Watching: From May to September, you can head out into the deep sea to spot Blue Whales and playful pods of Dolphins.

*	Water Sports: You can enjoy Jet Skiing, banana boat rides, and diving in the nearby reefs.

How to Get There

*	From Colombo: You can take a bus or the train to Trincomalee. The night mail train is a very popular and affordable option.

*	From Trincomalee: Nilaveli is about 15km from the main town. You can easily catch a local bus, hire a Tuk-Tuk, or take a taxi. The drive takes about 	20–30 minutes along the scenic coast.

Pro Tips for Your Trip

*	Best Time to Visit: The "Golden Season" for the East Coast is from May to September. The weather is sunny and the sea is at its calmest.

*	Eco-Friendly Travel: Since the area (especially Pigeon Island) is a protected marine park, please avoid using plastic and do not touch or step on 		the corals.

*	මුහුදේ නෑම: මෙහි මුහුද ගොඩක් වෙලාවට නිසලයි, ඒ නිසා බියක් නැතුව නෑමට සහ විවේකීව කාලය ගත කරන්න කියාපු තැන.

*	ජල ක්‍රීඩා: ජෙට් ස්කී (Jet Ski), බෝට්ටු සංචාර වගේ දේවල් වලටත් මෙතන ප්‍රසිද්ධයි.

*	කොළඹ සිට: කොළඹ ඉඳන් ත්‍රිකුණාමලයට බස් එකේ හෝ කෝච්චියේ එන්න පුළුවන්. (රාත්‍රී තැපැල් දුම්රිය ගොඩක් පහසුයි).
